About TekRocks IT Services

TekRocks IT Services offers comprehensive business solutions globally, covering ERP, SCM, PLM, WMS, CRM, portal, e-commerce, app development, project services, e-learning, training, staffing, and global sourcing.

Based in New Jersey, TekRocks IT Services operates globally, serving mid-market enterprises, Fortune 1000 companies, and government agencies. They specialize in driving business transformation and improving performance through advanced technology solutions, delivered by dedicated experts committed to customer satisfaction.

TekRocks IT Services provides an integrated portfolio of solutions and services, encompassing various technology and business practices. This approach offers a single point of accountability, reducing total cost of ownership and maximizing return on IT investments. The result is end-to-end delivery and support of comprehensive solutions, allowing clients to focus on driving competitive advantage in their core business.
