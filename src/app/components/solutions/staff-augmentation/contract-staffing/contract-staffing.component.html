CONTRACT STAFFING

Rapid technology changes and high attrition rates are major concerns for BPO and IT managers. Contract staffing offers companies tremendous flexibility to adjust team sizes based on project requirements, with TekRocks IT Services managing employee contracts. We ensure you access the best talent in your required skill set, with expertise in emerging technologies and next-generation trends.

TekRocks IT Services provides contracting staff on an hourly bill rate basis for the expected duration of the contract. This engagement model relieves you of all human resource functions, including payroll generation and distribution, payroll tax withholding, state and federal reporting requirements, and employer-related insurance administration.
