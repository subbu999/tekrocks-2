CONTRACT TO HIRE STAFFING

Contract-to-Hire staffing solutions offered by TekRocks IT Services allow our clients to transition a temporary employee into a full-time position after assessing their on-the-job performance. This intermediate staffing solution involves employing a candidate temporarily with the intention of offering them a permanent role after evaluating their skills and capabilities over a specific period. This approach offers employers flexibility in monitoring a consultant's work performance before extending a formal offer of employment.

TekRocks IT Services Contract-to-Hire solutions offer our customers the flexibility to hire candidates after an initial 3-6 month contract period. This arrangement allows clients to assess the candidate's core skills and on-the-job interpersonal abilities before committing to a permanent hire.

Contract-to-Hire Solutions operate on an hourly bill rate for the contract period until the consultant transitions to regular employment with the company. The arrangement may also involve a minimum number of hours that the consultant must work as a TekRocks IT Services Consultant before conversion.

TekRocks IT Services receives a conversion fee once the consultant transitions to regular employee status with the company.

At TekRocks IT Services, the process for candidate interviews, screening, and recruitment resembles that for temporary employees; however, candidates have a long-term goal, interest, and involvement in the company. TekRocks IT Services prioritizes employee development, investing in both core and soft skills to facilitate a seamless transition from contract resource to hired resource.
